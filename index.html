Famous Historical Landmarks

Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower in Paris, France. It was built from 1887 to 1889 as the entrance to the 1889 World's Fair. The tower has become a global cultural icon of France and one of the most recognizable structures in the world.

Learn more about Eiffel Tower

Great Wall of China

The Great Wall of China is a series of fortifications built along the northern borders of China to protect against invasions. The wall spans over 13,000 miles and was built between the 7th century BC and the 16th century.

Learn more about the Great Wall of China

Colosseum

The Colosseum is an ancient amphitheater in Rome, Italy, built between AD 70 and AD 80. It is the largest amphitheater ever built and was used for gladiatorial contests and public spectacles.
